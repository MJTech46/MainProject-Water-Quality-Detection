When to Take Immediate Action in Water Quality

  • Unusual taste or odor (metallic, chemical, rotten)
  • Visible discoloration or cloudiness
  • Sudden changes in pH or turbidity
  • Detection of harmful bacteria (E. coli, coliforms)
  • Excessive chemical contamination (nitrates, lead)
  • High levels of heavy metals (mercury, cadmium)
  • Unexpected spikes in conductivity or TDS
  • Algal blooms or biofilm growth
